# Generio Sitemap (Phase 0 — Locked)

**MVP language:** English only  
**Contact:** One form (no separate Brand Owner / Distributor forms)  
**Markets:** Interactive regional coverage map  

Word-doc pages **For Brand Owners**, **For Distributors**, and **News & Insights** are **deferred** — intent is captured via Enquiry Type on the single Contact form; news is post-MVP.

---

## Public sitemap

```text
/
├── /                         Home
├── /about                    About Generio
├── /services                 Services (listing)
│   └── /services/[slug]      Service detail
├── /markets                  Markets We Cover (interactive map)
├── /industries               Industries Served
├── /partners                 Partner Network
├── /success-stories          Success Stories / Testimonials
├── /contact                  Contact (single enquiry form)
├── /privacy-policy           Privacy Policy
├── /terms                    Terms and Conditions
└── /cookie-policy            Cookie Policy
```
