## Typography

Logo wordmark is a modern geometric sans-serif. Website stack (locked for implementation):

| Role | Font | Fallback |
|------|------|----------|
| Display / headings | **Plus Jakarta Sans** | `system-ui, sans-serif` |
| Body | **Source Sans 3** | `system-ui, sans-serif` |

Both are free Google Fonts, geometric, and readable for corporate marketing. Do not use Inter / Roboto / Arial as the primary brand face.
